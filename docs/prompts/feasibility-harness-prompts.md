# Feasibility Harness Prompts

These prompts define the initial Milestone 0 generated-input suite described in the canonical architecture.

## Tabletop Vignette Prompts

### P-001 Vintage Camera Still Life

An old silver film camera resting on a folded linen cloth on a wooden tabletop, soft window light, shallow depth of field, realistic materials, clean background.

### P-002 Ceramic Teapot Study

A matte ceramic teapot with two small cups on a dark oak table, gentle morning light, subtle steam, minimal background clutter, realistic studio photography.

### P-003 Botanical Desk Scene

A small potted succulent, brass scissors, and a leather notebook arranged on a tidy desk, warm side light, crisp focus on the objects, neutral backdrop.

### P-004 Fruit Bowl Composition

A shallow ceramic bowl with pears and plums on a stone surface, soft natural light, rich texture detail, restrained composition, realistic product-photo style.

### P-005 Mechanical Watch Display

A stainless steel wristwatch placed beside its open presentation box on a clean tabletop, dramatic but controlled lighting, realistic reflections, minimal scene.

### P-006 Reading Glasses and Book

A pair of round reading glasses resting on an open hardback book on a walnut table, quiet afternoon light, realistic paper texture, uncluttered background.

### P-007 Traveler's Desk Kit

A fountain pen, passport, and compact leather wallet arranged neatly on a tabletop, cinematic natural light, realistic materials, simple composition.

### P-008 Artisan Coffee Setup

A ceramic mug, small pour-over dripper, and coffee beans on a pale stone counter, soft diffuse light, realistic food-photography styling, controlled scene scale.

### P-009 Sewing Notions Layout

Thread spools, a small pincushion, and brass tailoring scissors arranged on a fabric-covered table, bright window light, realistic detail, clean backdrop.

### P-010 Pocket Objects Arrangement

A set of everyday carry items including a key, folding knife, and coin purse arranged on a wooden tabletop, top-down three-quarter perspective, realistic textures.

## Captured-Image Baselines

### B-001 Tabletop Object Baseline

Use a captured image of a single tabletop subject with limited background clutter and stable lighting.

### B-002 Small Multi-Object Baseline

Use a captured image of a small tabletop arrangement with 2 to 4 objects and controlled composition.

## Prompt Selection Notes

- keep scenes tabletop-scale
- prefer one dominant subject or a small coherent grouping
- avoid room-scale framing
- avoid heavy occlusion, mirrors, text-heavy surfaces, or dense background clutter
